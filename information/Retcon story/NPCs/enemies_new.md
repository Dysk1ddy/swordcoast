# CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Enemies are no longer “fantasy races.”

They are:
- system-corrupted beings
- engineered factions
- environmental adaptations
- remnants of Meridian infrastructure

# ACT 1 — FRONTIER ENEMIES

## 1. SCRAPLING RAIDER (Former: Goblin Skirmisher)

Light, fast scavengers formed from collapsed frontier societies.

- Role: early-game swarm / harassment
- Traits:
  - nimble movement
  - disengage behavior
- Lore:
  Scraplings are not a species—they are people reduced to survival patterns.

---

## 2. ASHFANG HOUND (Former: Wolf / Worg)

Mutated predators adapted to Emberway conditions.

- Role: pack hunter
- Traits:
  - pack tactics
  - knockdown attacks
- Lore:
  Exposure to route-residue altered their behavior and aggression.

---

## 3. ASHEN BRAND CUTTER (Former: Bandit)
