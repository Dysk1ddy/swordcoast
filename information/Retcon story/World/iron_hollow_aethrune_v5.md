# AETHRUNE — IRON HOLLOW (V5 — TRUE PARITY LOCATION BIBLE)

## OVERVIEW

Iron Hollow is a **living system under strain**.

Where Emberway is a system of movement, and Greywake is a system of truth,
Iron Hollow is a system of **human continuity**.

It persists through:
- labor
- memory
- negotiation
- sacrifice

Every structure, routine, and relationship exists because it is actively maintained.

---

# CORE TRUTH

> Iron Hollow does not stand because it is strong.  
> It stands because collapse has not yet won.

---

# HIDDEN LAYER (PARITY ADDITION)

Iron Hollow appears purely human-built.

But beneath it:
- old Meridian reinforcement grids subtly stabilize ground
- buried load-distribution layers reduce collapse risk
- drainage systems still partially function
- certain structural failures occur in patterns, not randomness

**Implication:**
Iron Hollow may survive because people keep it alive and because it sits on the edge of an old system that *still supports it.*

# MICRO-LOCATIONS (FULL EXPANSION)

## 1. THE SPLIT ANVIL
A broken forge station now used by multiple smiths.

- Ownership is shared but contested
- Tools are mismatched and passed between shifts
- Arguments here often reflect larger economic tension

**Use:**
- crafting hub
- labor disputes
- faction influence entry point

---

## 2. THE THIRD LANTERN
A lantern post that has never gone out.

- even during attacks
- even during shortages

Locals say:

**Use:**
- morale anchor
- symbolic quest location

---

## 3. THE WATER CUT
A narrow trench channeling runoff water.

- hand-maintained
- constantly clogging
- prone to collapse

**Use:**
- environmental hazard
- labor quests
- infrastructure failure events

## 4. THE BROKEN SCALE
An old weighing station used for ration disputes.
