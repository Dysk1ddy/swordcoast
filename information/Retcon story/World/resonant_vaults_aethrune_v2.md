# SYSTEM BEHAVIORS

## 1. PATTERN LEARNING

Vault identifies:
- repeated paths
- hesitation points
- safe-route reliance

Then:
- disrupts those paths
- introduces variation

---

## 2. ANTICIPATION

At higher awareness:

Vault begins to:
- close paths BEFORE player reaches them
- open misleading routes
- force adaptation

---

## 3. PERSONALIZED GEOMETRY

Different players would see:
- different layouts
- different valid paths

Even in same space.

---

## 4. MEMORY REACTION

Vault recalls:
- past player choices

And:
- recreates patterns
- punishes reliance

---

## 5. OBSERVATION PARADOX

Vault behaves differently when:
- directly observed
- ignored

Example:
- looking at door prevents it from opening
- turning away triggers change
