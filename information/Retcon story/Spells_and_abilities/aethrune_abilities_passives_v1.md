# CORE SYSTEM

## CHANNEL TYPES
All abilities are now “Channels”:

- Minor Channels (formerly cantrips)
- Standard Channels
- Overchannels (enhanced casts)

## RESOURCE SYSTEM
- Charge Bands (replaces spell slots)
- Focus (optional secondary resource for advanced builds)
