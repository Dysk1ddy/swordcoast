# VAROXI (The Forged)

## Overview
The Varoxi are a rare and often misunderstood people whose bodies are partially fused with remnants of Meridian Accord machinery.

They are not constructs.

They are not fully organic.

They are **living beings altered by system integration**—the result of catastrophic failures where flesh and machine became inseparable.

---

## Physical Appearance

### General Build
- Medium to tall height
- Broad or reinforced frames
- Movements can be precise but slightly mechanical in rhythm

### Skin & Integration
- Natural skin interwoven with metallic plating or growths
- Materials vary:
  - dark iron
  - bronze
  - blackened alloy
- Integration points appear at:
  - joints (elbows, knees)
  - spine
  - forearms
  - ribs or collarbone

### Eyes
- One or both eyes may be altered:
  - faint internal glow (amber, blue, or white)
  - mechanical iris structures in some individuals
- Unmodified eyes remain normal—but often sharper in focus

### Hair
- Often coarse or sparse near integration points
- Some Varoxi have areas where hair does not grow due to embedded systems

### Distinctive Trait: Resonance Hum
At close range, some Varoxi emit:
- a faint vibration
- or a low mechanical hum under certain conditions

This is not constant—but noticeable in silence.

---

## Origins

### Catastrophic Integration Events (Primary)

The Varoxi originate from individuals caught in:

- system overloads  
- relay collapses  
- power core ruptures  
- maintenance chamber failures  

During these events:
- emergency systems attempted to preserve life
- machinery merged with the body instead of stabilizing it

Those who survived became something new.

---

## Physiology

Varoxi bodies are hybrid systems.

### Structural Reinforcement
- Increased physical durability
- Reduced sensitivity to minor injuries

### Internal Interfaces
- Some components still function:
  - locking joints for stability
  - pressure regulation systems
  - energy conduction pathways

### Limitations
- Repairs are complex:
  - require both medical and mechanical knowledge
- Damage to integrated parts can affect the entire body

---

## Cultural Identity

Varoxi culture is not unified—but shaped by shared experience.

### Core Conflict: Identity vs Function

Every Varoxi must confront:

> “Am I still a person, or part of a system?”

---

### Community Tendencies
- Often isolate or form small enclaves
- Gather near ruins or old Accord sites
- Share knowledge of maintenance and survival

### Philosophies

#### 1. Reclaimers
- Seek to understand and control their integration
- View their condition as evolution

#### 2. Separatists
- Reject their mechanical aspects
- Attempt to live as “normally” as possible

#### 3. Instrumentalists
- Embrace function over identity
- See themselves as tools with purpose

---

## Behavior & Personality Tendencies

Common Varoxi traits:

- Highly analytical  
- Reserved and observant  
- Slow to trust  
- Practical in decision-making  

They often:
- assess situations before acting  
- avoid unnecessary risk  
- prioritize efficiency  

---

## Social Perception

Other races react strongly to Varoxi:

### Fear
- Seen as unnatural or unstable
- Association with failed systems

### Curiosity
- Studied by engineers and scholars
- Viewed as living relics

### Utility
- Valued for resilience and system interaction
- Used in dangerous or high-risk environments

---

## Relationship to Systems

Varoxi have a unique connection to Meridian infrastructure:

### Advantages
- Can interface with certain systems directly
- More resistant to system-based hazards
- Recognize mechanical patterns instinctively

### Risks
- Systems may attempt to “complete” them
- Old protocols may activate unexpectedly
- Overload can trigger loss of control

---

## Narrative Role

The Varoxi represent:

> The cost of becoming part of a system.

They are living proof that:

- the Accord did not just control the world  
- it changed the people within it  

---

## Gameplay Direction (Design Hook)

### Strengths
- Increased durability and damage resistance  
- Interaction with mechanical or system-based environments  
- Reduced impact from physical hazards  

### Weaknesses
- Vulnerability to system interference or overload  
- Complex healing requirements  
- Social penalties in certain interactions  

---

## Final Concept

The Varoxi are not broken.

They are unfinished.

> The system tried to preserve them—  
> and never stopped.
