# Roads That Remember  
## Original Universe Rewrite — Core Story Framework (V2: World + Factions)

---

# WORLD NAME

## The Continent of Aethrune

A fractured medieval land shaped not by kingdoms, but by **roads, records, and forgotten systems**.

Once unified under a vast infrastructural empire known as **The Meridian Accord**, Aethrune is now a broken frontier where power lies in controlling movement, information, and access.

---

# REGIONAL SETTING

## The Shatterbelt Frontier

Primary game setting.

A volatile stretch of land where:
- Old Accord infrastructure still partially functions
- Trade routes shift unpredictably
- Settlements cling to stability
- Competing factions fight to control routes and truth

---

# MAJOR LOCATIONS (ACT STRUCTURE)

## ACT 1 REGION — The Emberway

A scorched trade artery once maintained by the Accord.

Key locations:
- **Greywake Crossing** — a triage settlement built on collapsed road junctions  
- **Iron Hollow** — mining town struggling to stay supplied  
- **Cinderfall Ruins** — burned Accord relay facility  
- **Ashfall Watch** — fortified checkpoint turned faction stronghold  

---

## ACT 2 REGION — The Vein of Glass

A subterranean and mountainous region rich in ancient infrastructure.

Key locations:
- **Stonehollow Dig** — excavation site uncovering Accord systems  
- **Glasswater Intake** — water-control facility repurposed by factions  
- **Broken Prospect** — failed expedition zone  
- **The Black Lake Causeway** — partially submerged Accord transit path  

---

## ACT 3 REGION — The Meridian Depths

The buried heart of the old system.

- Massive infrastructural core
- Reality-warping signal systems
- The origin point of control and record

---

# FACTIONS

---

## 1. The Ashen Brand (Route Control Faction)

### Identity:
Militarized road syndicate.

### Goal:
Control all major routes across the Shatterbelt.

### Methods:
- Fake checkpoints
- Controlled ambushes
- Route manipulation
- Supply restriction

### Belief:
> “If you control the road, you control the outcome.”

### Role:
Primary antagonistic force in Act 1.

---

## 2. The Quiet Choir (Knowledge Faction)

### Identity:
Cult-like scholars and signal listeners.

### Goal:
Understand and harness the deeper systems beneath Aethrune.

### Methods:
- Rewriting records
- Intercepting information
- Repurposing Accord infrastructure
- Psychological manipulation

### Belief:
> “Truth is not found. It is tuned.”

### Role:
Primary Act 2 threat, secondary Act 1 presence.

---

## 3. The Free Caravans (Survivor Coalition)

### Identity:
Independent traders and settlements.

### Goal:
Maintain free movement and survive without domination.

### Methods:
- Informal alliances
- Shared resources
- Route knowledge

### Belief:
> “No road should belong to one master.”

### Role:
Player-aligned but fragmented faction.

---

## 4. The Ironbound Guild (Industrial Claimants)

### Identity:
Engineers, miners, and reclaimers of old Accord systems.

### Goal:
Restore infrastructure for profit and power.

### Methods:
- Excavation
- System reactivation
- Territorial claims

### Belief:
> “The past belongs to those who can rebuild it.”

### Role:
Act 2 political force.

---

## 5. The Lantern Faith (Stability Faction)

### Identity:
Religious order focused on preserving humanity and order.

### Goal:
Protect settlements and prevent collapse into chaos.

### Methods:
- Healing
- Mediation
- Moral authority

### Belief:
> “Light must remain, even if truth does not.”

### Role:
Stability anchor and moral counterweight.

---

# THE OLD EMPIRE

## The Meridian Accord

A lost civilization that:

- Designed and controlled all major routes
- Maintained unified infrastructure
- Recorded movement, trade, and decisions

### Key Systems:
- Road networks that adapt and shift
- Relay stations that transmit information
- Archives that store and rewrite records

### Collapse:
Unknown cause:
- Internal failure?
- External force?
- Intentional shutdown?

Remnants now:
- Partially active
- Dangerous
- Unpredictable

---

# WORLD RULES

## 1. Roads Are Systems
They are not just paths, but controlled structures.

## 2. Information Is Unstable
Records can be altered or corrupted.

## 3. Infrastructure Persists
Old systems still influence the present.

## 4. Control Is Layered
- Physical control (roads)
- Informational control (records)
- Structural control (systems)

---

# STORY INTEGRATION

- Act 1 = physical control (Ashen Brand)
- Act 2 = informational control (Quiet Choir)
- Act 3 = systemic control (Meridian core)
