# Roads That Remember  
## Original Universe Rewrite — Core Story Framework (V1)

---

# CORE PREMISE

This is not a story where the player discovers the world.

> This is a story where the world has already been structured — and the player is moving through it.

The roads are not neutral.

They remember.

---

# GENRE & TONE

- Dark fantasy / grounded frontier → evolving into abstract / cosmic systems
- Layered reality: physical world → systemic control → informational control
- Slow-burn mystery → structured revelation → existential confrontation

---

# CORE THEMES

## 1. Control vs Freedom
- Systems exist to reduce chaos
- But perfect systems remove choice

## 2. Knowledge as Power
- Information is not neutral
- Truth can be weaponized, hidden, or shaped

## 3. The Illusion of Discovery
- The player is not uncovering randomness
- They are navigating pre-designed pathways

## 4. Memory & Record
- What is remembered defines reality
- What is *unrecorded* may be the only true freedom

---

# WORLD OVERVIEW (ORIGINAL SETTING)

## The Frontier Region

A fractured trade frontier made of:
- Broken routes
- Half-abandoned settlements
- Old infrastructure from a prior civilization
- Competing factions trying to control movement and information

### Key Concept:
The region is held together not by rulers, but by **routes**.

Control the routes → control the world.

---

## The Old System (Pre-Collapse Civilization)

Long before the current era:
- A structured network governed movement, knowledge, and resource flow
- Roads, relay stations, archives, and signal systems formed a unified infrastructure
- This system collapsed or was intentionally dismantled

Remnants still exist:
- Buried facilities
- Broken signal systems
- Self-maintaining constructs
- Partial records that contradict each other

---

## The Current Era

The world is in a **partial recovery state**:
- Settlements survive, but lack full control
- Trade exists, but is unstable
- Knowledge is fragmented and unreliable

Multiple groups are attempting to:
- Rebuild
- Control
- Or exploit the remnants of the old system
