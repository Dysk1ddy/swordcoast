# AETHRUNE — SRD CLASS WHITELIST (V1)

## PURPOSE
Defines what class content is SAFE to use under SRD 5.2.1 (CC BY 4.0), what must be VERIFIED, and what must be REWRITTEN.

---

# SAFE TO KEEP (SRD-COMPLIANT)

## Core Classes (All Allowed)
- Barbarian
- Bard
- Cleric
- Druid
- Fighter
- Monk
- Paladin
- Ranger
- Rogue
- Sorcerer
- Warlock
- Wizard

✔ You can keep:
- Class names
- Core mechanics structure (levels, features, progression)
- Ability score system
- Combat system (d20, AC, etc.)

---

## Subclasses (Explicitly in SRD 5.2.1)
- Barbarian: Path of the Berserker
- Bard: College of Lore
- Cleric: Life Domain
- Druid: Circle of the Land
- Fighter: Champion
- Monk: Way of the Open Hand
- Paladin: Oath of Devotion
- Ranger: Hunter
- Rogue: Thief
- Sorcerer: Draconic Bloodline
- Warlock: Fiend Patron
- Wizard: School of Evocation

✔ These can be used as-is (with attribution)

---

## Core Systems (SAFE)
- Ability Scores (STR, DEX, etc.)
- Advantage / Disadvantage
- Proficiency Bonus
- Skills system
- Combat rules
- Spellcasting framework
- Equipment basics

---

# VERIFY BEFORE USING

## Subclasses NOT listed above
- Any additional subclass must be:
  - Confirmed in SRD 5.2.1
  OR
  - Rewritten into original content

---

## Spells
- Many spells are in SRD, but NOT ALL
✔ Action:
- Build a whitelist of allowed spells
- Remove or rename non-SRD spells

---

## Feats
- Some feats are SRD, others are not
✔ Action:
- Verify each feat individually

---

## Monsters / Enemies
- SRD includes many stat blocks
✔ Action:
- Use SRD stat blocks OR rewrite into original creatures

---

# MUST REWRITE / REMOVE

## BG3-Influenced Content
❌ NOT allowed to directly use:
- BG3-specific mechanics
- BG3 UI/wording
- BG3-exclusive abilities

---

## Non-SRD Subclasses / Features
❌ Examples:
- Hexblade (not SRD)
- Gloom Stalker (not SRD)
- Any expansion content

✔ Solution:
- Rename + redesign
- Keep gameplay role, change mechanics/lore

---

## Setting-Specific References
❌ Remove:
- Forgotten Realms lore
- DnD gods (Tymora, etc.)
- Official place names

✔ Replace with:
- Aethrune equivalents
