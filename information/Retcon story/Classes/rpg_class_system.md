The combat redesign splits hit chance from damage reduction. Agility and battlefield factors drive miss/hit chance; Defense reduces physical damage by percentage after a hit lands. Save-based spells can keep the current saving throw lane during migration.

## ⚔️ WARRIOR

Detailed warrior design now lives in `warrior_archetype_v1.md`. This short index keeps the role map visible.

### 🛡️ Tank — Juggernaut
- Builds Momentum when taking hits  
- Can taunt and reduce incoming damage  
- Releases stored damage as AoE  

**Identity:** Unstoppable frontline  

---

### ❤️ Heal — Bloodreaver
- Converts damage dealt into teamwide healing  
- Marks enemies so attacks restore ally HP  
- Stronger when playing aggressively  

**Identity:** Vampiric sustain through combat  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Berserker
- Gains damage as HP drops  
- Can sacrifice defense for burst  
- Lifesteal on aggressive attacks  

**Identity:** High-risk sustained damage  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Weapon Master
- Switches between weapon styles (cleave, pierce, crush)  
- Adapts to enemy weaknesses mid-fight  
- Builds combos for bonus effects  

**Identity:** Versatile, skill-based damage  

---

## 🔮 MAGE

Detailed mage design now lives in `mage_archetype_v1.md`. This short index keeps the role map visible.

### 🛡️ Tank — Spellguard
- Converts mana into shields  
- Reflects or absorbs incoming spells  
- Can anchor wards to draw attacks and reduce ally damage nearby  

**Identity:** Magical damage sponge  

---

### ❤️ Heal — Aethermancer
- Converts mana into healing fields and bursts  
- Excess healing becomes temporary shields  
- Can overcharge allies with bonus HP  

**Identity:** Resource-driven support  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Arcanist
- Builds arcane charge through spell combos  
- Releases massive burst abilities  
- Rewards planning and sequencing  

**Identity:** Setup → explosion gameplay  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Elementalist
- Controls fire, ice, lightning  
- Combines elements for bonus effects  
- Terrain interaction (lingering damage zones)  

**Identity:** Flexible AoE and control damage  

---

## 🗡️ ROGUE

Detailed rogue design now lives in `rogue_archetype_v1.md`. This short index keeps the role map visible.

### 🛡️ Tank — Shadowguard
- Dodge/evasion-based tank  
- Uses decoys and misdirection  
- Gains defense when avoiding attacks  

**Identity:** Tank through avoidance  

---

### ❤️ Heal — Alchemist
- Throws potions for healing and buffs  
- Creates AoE healing zones  
- Mixes effects (heal + resist, heal + speed)  

**Identity:** Utility-heavy support  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Assassin
- Massive burst from stealth  
- Bonus vs isolated or low-HP targets  
- Resets or chains kills  

**Identity:** Single-target eliminator  

---

### ⚔️ DPS — Poisoner
- Applies stacking damage-over-time effects  
- Can detonate stacks for burst  
- Weak upfront, lethal over time  

**Identity:** Attrition and control  

---

## ⚖️ System Notes
- Each archetype supports Tank / Heal / DPS / DPS  
- Roles are active and distinct  
- Designed for clarity in text-based combat  

---

## 🔥 Optional Balance Tweaks
- Bloodreaver heals less when not attacking  
- Poisoner enables bonus damage from allies on poisoned targets
