# Act 2 Enemy Expansion Draft

This draft adds fifteen Act 2 enemy concepts built around the act's core theme: control of truth, dangerous knowledge, false authority, and listening-horror spreading upward from Resonant Vaults.

These are intentionally tuned above Act 1 fodder. The opening of Act 2 should already feel like the party is fighting trained saboteurs, warped cave specialists, corrupted Meridian guardians, and Quiet Choir elites rather than disposable goblins or wolves.

## Balance Target

- Early Act 2 pressure: level 4 baseline enemies with roughly 38-45 HP, AC 14-16, and one strong battlefield ability
- Mid Act 2 pressure: level 5 elites with roughly 48-58 HP, AC 15-16, and two layered abilities
- Late Act 2 pressure: level 6 finishers with roughly 58-72 HP, AC 16-17, and one encounter-warping signature move
- Damage target: most baseline attacks should sit around `1d8+2` to `2d6+2`; burst abilities can spike harder if limited-use

## Early Act 2 Pressure

### False-Map Skirmisher

- Archetype: `false_map_skirmisher`
- Base sheet: level 4, 38 HP, AC 15, Route Knife `1d8+3`, XP 175, Gold 14
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `skirmisher`, `parley`; high DEX and Stealth
- Signature abilities:
  - `route_splice` once: shifts position, gains `emboldened` 1, and leaves behind false trail markers; first enemy ally attacking the same target this round gains `+2` to hit
  - `mislead_step` once: DEX save DC 13 or target becomes `reeling` 1 as the skirmisher cuts from an unexpected angle
- Theme fit: turns map control itself into a weapon; the enemy wins by making the party trust the wrong route
- Suggested locations: sabotage night, woodland route fights, delayed-lead recovery scenes

### Claimbinder Notary

- Archetype: `claimbinder_notary`
- Base sheet: level 4, 40 HP, AC 15, Seal Mace `1d6+3`, XP 180, Gold 22
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `leader`, `parley`
- Signature abilities:
  - `seizure_order` once: WIS save DC 14 or target loses `emboldened`/`blessed` style boons and suffers `reeling` 2
  - `filed_objection` once: marks one target; allies deal +2 damage against that target until the notary is hit
- Theme fit: a legal-theater enemy who weaponizes authority, paper legitimacy, and the act's claims-war tension
- Suggested locations: claims council fallout, Exchange or Ironbound sponsor conflict, rival expedition command cells

### Echo Sapper

- Archetype: `echo_sapper`
- Base sheet: level 4, 42 HP, AC 14, Breach Hammer `1d10+2`, XP 190, Gold 16
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `siege`
- Signature abilities:
  - `survey_charge` once: detonates unstable ground; DEX save DC 14 or takes `2d6` force and falls `prone`
  - `dust_blind` once: creatures hit by the blast also risk `blinded` 1 if already `prone` or `reeling`
- Theme fit: fits the expedition war by making tunnel integrity and route access part of the fight
- Suggested locations: Stonehollow, Broken Prospect, blocked adits, collapsing side passages

### Meridian Archive Warden

- Archetype: `pact_archive_warden`
- Base sheet: level 4, 44 HP, AC 16, Archive Spear `1d8+3`, XP 200, Gold 18
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `construct`, `sentinel`
- Signature abilities:
  - `access_denied` once: projects a ward line; STR save DC 14 or target is shoved back and `slowed` for 1 round
  - `custody_protocol` passive: gains +2 AC against the first attack each round from a target it already struck
- Theme fit: represents the old Meridian Compact trying to guard knowledge badly, rigidly, and without human judgment
- Suggested locations: Broken Prospect, sealed record rooms, Resonant Vault archive thresholds

### Blackglass Listener

- Archetype: `blackglass_listener`
- Base sheet: level 4, 41 HP, AC 15, Thought Spike `1d8+2` psychic, XP 190, Gold 10
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `aberration`, `scout`
- Signature abilities:
  - `overhear_intent` once: WIS save DC 14 or the target becomes `frightened` 1 and cannot gain advantage-like bonuses until its next turn
  - `feedback_pulse` once: deals `2d6` psychic to a target that used a spell or support action last round
- Theme fit: this is dangerous knowledge made predatory; it hunts thought before action
- Suggested locations: Hushfen warning scenes, Blackglass outskirts, high-whisper route events

## Mid Act 2 Pressure

### Choir Cartographer

- Archetype: `choir_cartographer`
- Base sheet: level 5, 48 HP, AC 15, Survey Blade `1d8+3`, XP 225, Gold 28
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `leader`, `parley`
- Signature abilities:
  - `map_the_weakness` once: chooses a target; all allies gain `+2` damage against that target for 2 rounds
  - `stolen_route` once: swaps places with a conscious ally and gains `blessed` 1, representing perfect positional knowledge
- Theme fit: embodies the act's fixation on who gets to define the map and therefore define reality on the ground
- Suggested locations: sabotage night command variant, late early-route convergence, Resonant Vault entry teams

### Resonance Leech

- Archetype: `resonance_leech`
- Base sheet: level 5, 50 HP, AC 14, Siphon Tendril `2d6+2`, XP 230, Gold 0
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `aberration`
- Signature abilities:
  - `drain_cadence` once: CON save DC 14 or target takes `2d6` psychic and loses 1 active positive status; the leech gains 8 temp HP
  - `echo_feast` passive: deals +`1d6` damage to `frightened`, `reeling`, or `silenced` targets
- Theme fit: literalizes the idea that leaked resonance becomes a feeding ecology
- Suggested locations: South Adit contamination pockets, Resonant Vault side caverns, Blackglass feeder rooms

### Survey-Chain Revenant

- Archetype: `survey_chain_revenant`
- Base sheet: level 5, 52 HP, AC 16, Chain Pick `1d10+3`, XP 240, Gold 20
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `undead`, `bruiser`
- Signature abilities:
  - `drag_to_marker` once: STR save DC 15 or target is `grappled` and pulled into melee range
  - `unfinished_shift` passive: while grappling a target, the revenant gains resistance-style damage reduction of 2 from weapon hits
- Theme fit: an undead laborer or foreman whose death never released it from duty, only from mercy
- Suggested locations: delayed rescue routes, South Adit, broken rail lines, burial ledgers

### Iron Censer Horror

- Archetype: `censer_horror`
- Base sheet: level 5, 55 HP, AC 16, Smothering Swing `2d6+2`, XP 250, Gold 18
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `construct`, `horror`
- Signature abilities:
  - `hush_smoke` once: CON save DC 14 or targets in a small area suffer `blinded` 1 and deal -2 damage with verbal or morale-style abilities
  - `cinder_liturgy` passive: enemies starting adjacent to it while `frightened` take `1d4` fire
- Theme fit: a ritual machine that turns religious ceremony into suffocation and sensory denial
- Suggested locations: cult quiet-hall rooms, Meridian Forge approach, sanctum profanation encounters

### Memory-Taker Adept

- Archetype: `memory_taker_adept`
- Base sheet: level 5, 46 HP, AC 15, Quietus Dagger `1d8+3`, XP 220, Gold 24
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `assassin`, `parley`
- Signature abilities:
  - `erase_witness` once: WIS save DC 14 or target loses reaction-style defensive options and suffers `reeling` 2
  - `borrowed_instinct` once: after hitting a target, the adept gains `emboldened` 2 if that target had any positive status
- Theme fit: one of the cleanest "control of truth" enemies, built around deleting testimony and stealing tactical certainty
- Suggested locations: Quiet Choir kill teams, sponsor assassination attempts, Blackglass cult cells

## Late Act 2 Pressure

- Base sheet: level 6, 58 HP, AC 16, Choir Staff `1d8+4`, XP 300, Gold 34
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `humanoid`, `leader`, `parley`
- Signature abilities:
  - `shard_hymn` once: WIS save DC 15 or takes `3d6` psychic, `frightened` 2, and `reeling` 2
  - `choral_screen` once: grants self and allies 6 temp HP and clears `frightened` from cult allies
- Theme fit: a senior Quiet Choir field theologian who turns doctrine into battlefield acoustics
- Suggested locations: Meridian Forge antechambers, high-whisper branches, elite cult escort fights

### Blackglass Adjudicator

- Archetype: `blacklake_adjudicator`

- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `construct`, `leader`, `sentinel`
- Signature abilities:

  - `mirror_verdict` passive: the first ranged hit against it each round reflects `1d6` force back to the attacker
- Theme fit: a near-final guardian representing the Meridian Compact's belief that dangerous thresholds require judgment before entry
- Suggested locations: Blackglass causeway, ritual bridgeheads, threshold rooms before the Meridian Forge

### Forge-Echo Stalker

- Signature abilities:

- Theme fit: what happens when the mine starts answering back instead of humming in the walls
- Suggested locations: Resonant Vault deeper chambers, Meridian Forge side halls, whisper-surged boss support packs

### Covenant Breaker Wight

- Archetype: `covenant_breaker_wight`

- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `undead`, `leader`
- Signature abilities:
  - `break_the_line` once: targets a marked defender or support character; STR or WIS save DC 15 or suffers `prone` plus `frightened` 1
  - `life_levy` passive: regains 6 HP the first time each round it damages a `blessed` or `emboldened` target
- Theme fit: a fallen Meridian Compact officer who now exists to punish loyalty, vows, and coordinated resistance
- Suggested locations: South Adit climax, oathbound tombs, late Broken Prospect, Meridian Meridian Forge guard retinues

### Hollowed Survey Titan

- Archetype: `hollowed_survey_titan`
- Base sheet: level 6, 72 HP, AC 15, Survey Maul `2d8+4`, XP 375, Gold 30
- Tags and traits: `enemy`, `construct`, `brute`
- Signature abilities:
  - `bearing_collapse` once: smashes the battlefield; DEX save DC 15 or targets in a short line take `3d6` bludgeoning and become `prone`
  - `loadbearing_frame` passive: while above half HP, forced movement against the titan automatically fails
- Theme fit: a terrifying expedition-machine body built from survey frames, mine braces, and corrupted route hardware
- Suggested locations: Broken Prospect finale, Resonant Vault labor vaults, Meridian Forge boss-support variant

## Encounter Use Guidance

- Early Act 2 should mix one level 4 enemy with one existing level 2-3 support enemy rather than opening with a full elite stack.
- Mid Act 2 can field one level 5 enemy plus one level 3-4 support, or two level 4s working together.
- Late Act 2 can support one level 6 elite plus one level 4-5 support enemy in major fights.
- Avoid using more than one battlefield-lock piece in the same standard encounter unless that fight is meant to feel like a miniboss.
- If the party enters a route late, prefer enemies that punish bad information:
  - `false_map_skirmisher`
  - `claimbinder_notary`
  - `choir_cartographer`
  - `memory_taker_adept`
- If whisper pressure is high, prefer enemies that reward `reeling`, `frightened`, or psychic attrition:
  - `blackglass_listener`
  - `resonance_leech`
  - `obelisk_chorister`
  - `forge_echo_stalker`
